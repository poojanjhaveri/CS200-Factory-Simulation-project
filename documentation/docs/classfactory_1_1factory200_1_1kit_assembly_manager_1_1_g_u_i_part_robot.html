Detailed Description

Robot that creates kits using parts from the lane nests.

The GUIPartRobot obtains parts from the nest and places it into the working kit. It takes orders from the Kit Assembly Manager. It has pickup functionality to transport parts along its arm to the working kit It moves along a rail and only stops at one of eight preset positions facing a lane the robot arm rotates along the rail to face the lane at the optimal angle

Orders 0-7 - pick up part nest 0-7 8 - drop parts onto kit 1 9 - drop parts onto kit 0 ... 10-17 - move to nest 0-7 18 - move to kit0 19 - move to kit1 20 - move to default
