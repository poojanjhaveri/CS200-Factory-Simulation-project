Detailed Description

Robot that moves kits in the KitWorkingArea.

The GuiKitRobot handles the moving of kits in the kit assembly area. It takes orders from the Kit Assembly Manager. The GuiKitRobot must also communicate with the KitDelivery manager to obtain and deliver kits It has pickup functionality to move entire kits The robot is capable of rotating along the base and extending the arm, but is incapable of moving from its base.
