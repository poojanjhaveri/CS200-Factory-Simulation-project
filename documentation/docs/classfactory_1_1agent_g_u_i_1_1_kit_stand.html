Detailed Description

keeps track of what is on the kit stand

The KitStand keeps track of what is on the kit stand Objects that are placed on the kit stand are empty kits, partially full kits, and finished kits the kit stand communicates with the parts robot, kit robot and the camera to see if a kit is full and should be sent off
